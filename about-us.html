ABOUT US

Kerepek R Us is a start up business founded by a small group of college students in 2021 based in Selangor. We strive to offer our customers the very best when it comes to local snacks.

Our business initially started from a group project but since then, we've only grown bigger and better! We have expanded out horizons and the variety of our menu.

We've gone from selling kerepek to college friends and family to taking orders from all over the state of Selangor. We now have agents in many towns and cities so that more of Malaysia can have access to our delicious snacks at reasonable prices! Our current objective is to be well known through out Malaysia and grow our reach all the way to Sabah and Sarawak.
